My Story

Hello, I am Alexi Valadez.

I'm a college student and photographer with a deep love for the natural world and the stories that light can tell. Growing up surrounded by wide open landscapes shaped the way I see — unhurried, observant, always looking for the frame within the frame. My camera is how I make sense of the world around me.

When I'm not shooting, I'm studying, exploring trails, or experimenting with new editing techniques. This site is my corner of the internet — part portfolio, part blog, part small business — and I'm glad you found it. Every image here was made with intention and a whole lot of heart.
